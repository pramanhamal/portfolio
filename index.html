Artifact 1 — Evolution of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

This artifact presents a timeline of major milestones that shaped the development of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Understanding the history of AI helps technology leaders better understand modern systems and future innovations.

AI & ML Timeline

1956 — Birth of Artificial Intelligence

The Dartmouth Conference formally introduced AI as a field.

1980s — Expert Systems

Rule-based systems used in healthcare and finance.

1997 — Deep Blue

IBM’s Deep Blue defeated a world chess champion.

2012 — Deep Learning Breakthrough

Neural networks advanced image and speech recognition.

2017 — Transformers

Enabled modern natural language processing.

2020–Present — Generative AI

AI systems generate text, images, and code.
