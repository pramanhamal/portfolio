Artifact 1 — Evolution of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

This artifact presents a timeline of major milestones that shaped the development of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Understanding the history of AI helps technology leaders better understand modern systems and future innovations.

AI & ML Timeline

1956 — Birth of Artificial Intelligence

The Dartmouth Conference formally introduced the term Artificial Intelligence, marking the beginning of AI as a scientific research field.

1980s — Expert Systems

Expert systems became early AI applications that used rule-based logic to assist with decision-making in industries such as healthcare and finance.

1997 — Deep Blue Defeats World Chess Champion

IBM's Deep Blue defeated Garry Kasparov, demonstrating the ability of machines to outperform humans in complex strategic games.

2012 — Deep Learning Breakthrough

Neural networks achieved major improvements in image recognition and pattern detection, leading to rapid progress in computer vision and speech recognition.

2017 — Transformer Architecture

Transformers changed natural language processing and enabled the development of modern large language models used today.

2020–Present — Generative AI Era

Generative AI systems can create text, images, code, and other content, transforming industries and accelerating innovation worldwide.
